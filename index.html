‚ö° Purple Sectors

A Purple Sector is awarded when you set the fastest time in a specific sector (Sector 1, 2, or 3) within a round, regardless of your overall position. You can earn up to 3 purple sectors per round (one for each sector).

Note: 1st place drivers don't receive bonus points for purple sectors, but they are still tracked!
