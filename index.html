üìú Rules & Scoring System

üèÅ How It Works

  • Complete your lap and record your sector times
  • Submit your times through the form
  • You can submit multiple attempts per round
  • Only your fastest lap per round counts for points
  • Purple sectors are awarded for the fastest time in each individual sector

üéØ Points System

ü•á 1st Place: 10 points (no purple sector bonus)
ü•à 2nd Place: 6 points + 1 point per purple sector (max 2)
ü•â 3rd Place: 4 points + 2 points per purple sector (max 2)

‚ö° Purple Sectors

A Purple Sector is awarded when you set the fastest time in a specific sector (Sector 1, 2, or 3) within a round, regardless of your overall position. You can earn up to 3 purple sectors per round (one for each sector).

Note: 1st place drivers don't receive bonus points for purple sectors, but they are still tracked!
